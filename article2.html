The automobile spare parts industry in Sri Lanka is vast and growing, offering a number of ways for customers to purchase parts — from retail shops to online platforms.

The Growing Industry

The vehicle industry of Sri Lanka has also offered opportunities for potential exporters. The spare parts sector is supported by strong household purchasing power, making it one of the fastest-growing markets in the region.

Ways to Purchase Spare Parts

There are three common ways to purchase spare parts in Sri Lanka:

  • Visit multiple retail shops.
  • Order parts online and have them delivered to your doorstep.
  • Purchase through online classifieds.

Retail & After-Sales Service

The automobile spare part industry in Sri Lanka is divided into two major segments:

  • Retail Sector: Supply of genuine OEM and after-market spare parts.
  • After-Sales Service: Repair, maintenance, and replacement of automobile components.
