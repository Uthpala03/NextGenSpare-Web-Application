    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is a Discovery Point?

    A local partner location where you can pick up online orders, get basic fitment guidance, and drop off returns.

    Can I pay on pickup?

    Yes, select “Cash on Pickup” at checkout where available at your chosen point.

    How long will my order be held?

    Orders are held for 5 business days unless extended via support.
