Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your luxury auto parts purchase. Please review our comprehensive return policy below.

Return Policy Overview

At NextGenSpare.lk, we understand the importance of getting the right parts for your luxury vehicle. We offer a comprehensive return policy to ensure your complete satisfaction with every purchase.

Important Notice

All returns must be initiated within 30 days of purchase and must meet our return conditions outlined below.

Eligible Returns

What Can Be Returned:

  • Unused parts in original packaging
  • Parts with manufacturing defects
  • Incorrect parts shipped due to our error
  • Parts that don't match the original specifications
  • Damaged parts received during shipping

Return Conditions:

  • Parts must be in original, unopened packaging
  • All original labels, tags, and documentation must be intact
  • Parts must be in resalable condition
  • Original receipt or order confirmation required
  • Parts must not show signs of installation or use

Non-Returnable Items

For safety and hygiene reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Fluids and oils (engine oil, brake fluid, coolant, etc.)
  • Filters that have been opened or used
  • Electrical parts that have been installed
  • Custom-ordered or special-request parts
  • Parts ordered more than 6 months ago
  • Consumable items (wipers, bulbs, fuses)
  • Interior items that have been installed

Return Process

Step 1: Contact Us

Contact our customer service team within 30 days of purchase. Provide your order number and reason for return.

Step 2: Return Authorization

We'll provide you with a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and return instructions.

Step 3: Package and Ship

Securely package the item in its original packaging and include the RMA number. Ship the item back to our designated address.

Step 4: Inspection and Refund

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Refund Information

Refund Methods:

  • Original payment method (credit card, bank transfer)
  • Store credit for future purchases
  • Exchange for correct part (if applicable)

Refund Timeline:

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your bank or credit card company.

Shipping Costs

Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product. Customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless otherwise specified.

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for:

  • Wrong parts shipped due to our error
  • Defective or damaged parts
  • Size or specification changes (subject to availability)

Exchange requests must be made within 30 days of purchase and follow the same return process outlined above.

Warranty Claims

For warranty-related issues:

  • OEM parts come with manufacturer warranty
  • Contact us immediately if you receive a defective part
  • Warranty claims are processed separately from returns
  • Installation-related issues may void warranty

Special Circumstances

Bulk Orders:

Orders over LKR 100,000 may have special return conditions. Contact our sales team for details.

International Orders:

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs and any customs fees.

Emergency Orders:

Rush or emergency orders may have modified return policies due to expedited processing.
